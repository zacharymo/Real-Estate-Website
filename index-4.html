About Our Resources

With over 13 years as an accomplished Realtor, Dimex has been Excellent and Progressive

As an award-winning Realtor ® and CEO of The Dimex Real Estate Group, Nigerias’s daily business activities include counseling modest first-time home buyers to quarterbacking the listing/marketing of homes owned by corporate leaders, celebrities, physicians and investors throughout Africa. With comprehensive market knowledge, exceptional personal service, and superior negotiation skills, we are the unparalleled choice for your next real estate transaction. With over 13 years as an accomplished Realtor, Dimex has been named “Nigeria’s most beloved real estate agent” as host of HGTV’s top-rated show, Property Virgins, now in its twelfth season, and the forthcoming Flipping Heros. We are made recurring appearances as the real estate “go-to firm” on NBC’s Today Show, TVC, CHANNELS, AIT, NTA as well as being featured in Rolling Stone, Black Enterprise, Fast Company, and a host of other magazines. Dimex is the author of “Keep Cool…It’s Just Real Estate: Your No-Stress Guide To Buying A Home,” on Running Press/Perseus Books.Licensed in two states, Dimex is also the real estate columnist for Abuja Magazine’s HOME. A powerhouse speaker and moderator, Dimex hosted the stage at Mo Abdus’s “The Life You Want Tour”, and countless business conferences. As a 20 year award winning radio personality, Dimex has been syndicated in more than 60 cities throughout the Africa. “My background in broadcasting and branding has proven to be invaluable,” says Dimex. “It has given me a strong foundation and a unique understanding of how to make people pay attention to my unique value proposition and garner RESULTS! You can count on me to help you successfully navigate your home sale or purchase and to provide you with the insights you need so you can make the best, most informed decisions.”
